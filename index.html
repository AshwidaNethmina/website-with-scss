Goals of Data Security

  • Confientiality
    •

    Assets can be only accessed by authorized people

  • Intergrity
    •

    Assets can be only changed by authorized people

  • Availabiliti
    •

    Assets can be accessed when required

  • Authentication
    •

    Ability of system to can confirm the identity of a sender

  • Non-redudiation
    •

    Ability of system to can confirm that a sender cannot convincingly deny doing something
